# Hello, I'm Daniel Zunarelli

I’m a Data Engineer with over 4 years of experience in the data domain, including over a year specializing in data engineering. My background includes working with marketing analytics, pharmaceutical ERP data, and consulting projects in the financial services and insurance sectors, providing a broad perspective on transforming raw data into reliable datasets for analytics and decision-making.

In my data engineering experience, I have worked on large-scale data platform migrations and pipeline development in cloud environments. In the financial sector, I contributed to the migration of more than 600 Oracle PL/SQL and Informatica PowerCenter processes to Databricks, translating complex business logic into PySpark, Python, and SQL while processing datasets ranging from millions to billions of records. I also implemented data validation and observability mechanisms to ensure data quality and consistency during migration, while optimizing workloads to reduce processing latency by nearly 40%.

In the insurance sector, I have worked on migrating analytics workloads from SAS to Google Cloud Platform (GCP), building and orchestrating ETL/ELT pipelines using Apache Airflow and dbt, and optimizing queries to improve pipeline performance by an average of 70%. My work also includes version control with GitLab and collaboration through Jira in agile data engineering teams.

**Cloud Platforms:** AWS, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Databricks, Oracle, dbt
